A Legacy of Heavy Industry

ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК" has evolved from a regional midstream logistics provider into a vertically integrated energy major. Our timeline reflects a continuous reinvestment of capital into deep-conversion refining and secure distribution infrastructure.

2002

Foundation & Capitalization

Incorporation of ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК". Commenced operations as a domestic logistics coordinator managing hydrocarbon distribution across the Russian Federation with primary regional hubs.

2008

Midstream Infrastructure Surge

Strategic acquisition and construction of high-capacity tank storage facilities (TSA). Successfully integrated primary refinery trunk pipelines into our growing logistics network.

2014

Maritime Export Integration

Pivoted to international markets. Secured sovereign export quotas and established global supply lines for Aviation Kerosene (JP54) and Virgin Fuel Oil (D6) through deep-water port terminals.

2019

Deep-Conversion Modernization

Commissioned advanced Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC) and Hydro-treating units. Increased output of Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel (EN590 10ppm) to meet rigorous Euro-5 and Euro-6 specifications.

2026

Sustainable Energy & LNG Frontier

Launched cryogenic liquefaction (LNG) strategy leveraging the Northern Sea Route (NSR) to deliver low-carbon energy to the global market, reaching peak operational efficiency through automated governance.
