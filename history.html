A Legacy of Heavy Industry

ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК" has evolved from a regional midstream logistics provider into a vertically integrated energy major. Our timeline reflects a continuous reinvestment of capital into deep-conversion refining.

2002

Foundation & Capitalization

Incorporation of ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК". Commenced operations as a domestic logistics coordinator managing distribution across the Russian Federation.

2008

Midstream Infrastructure Surge

Acquisition and construction of high-capacity tank storage facilities (TSA). Integrated primary refinery pipelines to our logistics network.

2014

Maritime Export Integration

Pivoted to international markets. Secured sovereign export quotas and established supply lines for Aviation Kerosene and Virgin Fuel Oil (D6).

2019

Deep-Conversion Modernization

Commissioned advanced Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC) units. Increased output of Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel (EN590 10ppm) to Euro-5 specs.

2024

Sustainable Energy & LNG Frontier

Launched cryogenic liquefaction (LNG) strategy leveraging the Northern Sea Route (NSR) to deliver low-carbon energy to the global market.
