A Legacy of Heavy Industry

Established at the turn of the millennium, ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК" has evolved from a regional midstream logistics provider into a vertically integrated, multi-billion dollar energy major. Our timeline reflects a continuous reinvestment of capital into deep-conversion refining technologies, maritime export capabilities, and sovereign reserve acquisitions.

2002

Foundation & Capitalization

Incorporation of ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК" under OGRN 1027739129186. The company commenced operations primarily as an inland logistics and rail-freight coordinator, managing the distribution of raw crude oil and heavy fuel oil (Mazut) to domestic power generation facilities across the Russian Federation.

2008

Midstream Infrastructure Surge

Executed a massive capital expenditure program to acquire and construct high-capacity tank storage facilities (Tank Storage Agreements). Integrated primary refinery pipelines to our logistics network, allowing for the stable aggregation of export-volume reserves at key maritime terminals.

2014

Maritime Export Integration

Successfully pivoted to international downstream markets. BTK Group secured sovereign export quotas and began large-scale loading of Aviation Kerosene (JP-54) and Virgin Fuel Oil (D6) at major deep-water ports, establishing direct supply lines to the Asia-Pacific and European commodity markets.

2019

Deep-Conversion Modernization

A landmark year for our refining assets. Successfully commissioned advanced Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC) and hydrotreating units, significantly increasing our Nelson Complexity Index. This allowed BTK to produce Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel (EN590 10ppm) in absolute compliance with stringent Euro-5 and international environmental standards.

2024

Sustainable Energy & LNG Frontier

Attained comprehensive ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certifications across all operational divisions. Expanding into the natural gas sector, BTK launched its cryogenic liquefaction (LNG) strategy, leveraging the Northern Sea Route (NSR) to deliver high-density, low-carbon energy to the global market while maintaining zero-flaring ESG directives.
