The Governing Authority

The Board of Directors of ООО "АВТОДОРТРЕЙД-БТК" serves as the highest strategic authority. Operating at the intersection of sovereign energy security and international commodity markets, the Board's mandate is to ensure the long-term capitalization of the enterprise.

The Board is responsible for sanctioning multi-billion dollar capital expenditure programs and overseeing international TSA logistics frameworks.
