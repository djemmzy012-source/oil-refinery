Technical Mastery

Advanced Hydrotreating Units

Our primary refinery complex utilizes the proprietary T-Star hydrocracking process. This allows us to remove 99.9% of nitrogen and sulfur compounds from crude feedstock, ensuring Euro-6 compliance and minimal environmental impact across our entire logistics network.

Thermal Oxidation

Utilizing high-temperature closed-loop oxidizers to eliminate VOC emissions by 98.4% across all refining and storage operations.

Zero-Discharge Cycle

Our industrial water treatment facility returns 84% of process water to the cooling loops, minimizing freshwater consumption.

Catalytic Desulfurization

Advanced hydrotreating removes 99.9% of sulfur compounds, producing ultra-low sulfur diesel and gasoline meeting Euro-6 standards.

Carbon Capture
