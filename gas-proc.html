H2S Removal

Cryogenic Expansion

Temperatures below -120°C condense ethane, propane, and butane from methane stream using turbo-expander technology for maximum NGL recovery.

Amine Treating

Aqueous MDEA solutions strip H2S and CO2 yielding safe, sweet natural gas meeting international pipeline specifications for export.

Dehydration

Molecular sieve beds remove water vapor to prevent hydrate formation and corrosion in downstream pipeline infrastructure.

Sulfur Recovery

Claus process converts removed H2S into elemental sulfur (99.9% purity) for fertilizer and chemical industry applications.

Processing Workflow

Inlet Separation

Raw gas enters through high-pressure separators removing free liquids, solids, and condensates before processing begins.

Acid Gas Removal

Amine contactors strip hydrogen sulfide and carbon dioxide to meet pipeline quality standards and environmental regulations.

Cryogenic Cooling

Gas is chilled to -120°C using propane pre-cooling and turbo-expander refrigeration for NGL condensation.

Fractional Distillation

De-ethanizer, de-propanizer, and de-butanolizer columns separate NGLs into specification-grade ethane, propane, and butane products.
