Standard TSA Operating Procedure

1

ICPO & Corporate Vetting

Buyer submits ICPO and Company Profile. Legal desk conducts KYC/AML compliance checks.

2

Commercial Invoice & SGA

BTK issues CI and SGA. Buyer countersigns to lock in pricing formulas.

3

Tank Storage Agreement (TSA)

Buyer provides TSA from an authorized terminal. BTK validates coordinates and signs endorsement.

4

Injection & Dip Test

BTK issues Injection Schedule and unconditional Dip Test Authorization (DTA).

5

SGS / BV Audit

Buyer’s inspector (SGS/Intertek) conducts deep molecular analysis of product in shore tanks.

6

Final Settlement (MT103)

Upon SGS success, buyer transfers funds via MT103 against shipping documents and Title.

7

Product Lifting

Title is transferred. Buyer's vessel (cleared via Q88) attaches to manifold and injections begin.
