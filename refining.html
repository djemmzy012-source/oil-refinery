Primary & Secondary Conversion

VDU Distillation

Atmospheric and Vacuum Distillation (CDU/VDU) separating crude by thermal boiling points into primary fractions.

Catalytic Cracking

Breaking long-chain molecules into high-value products like Jet JP54 and EN590 Diesel using advanced zeolite catalysts.

Hydrotreating Units

Deep desulfurization processes utilizing high-pressure hydrogen to eliminate sulfur, nitrogen, and aromatic compounds.

Delayed Coking

Thermally cracking residual heavy bottoms (vacuum residue) into lighter, more valuable distillates and petroleum coke.

Environmental & Flare Management

Our facilities integrate cutting-edge Sulfur Recovery Units (SRU) achieving 99.9% extraction efficiency, alongside closed-loop flare gas recovery systems. This guarantees Euro-5 emission compliance and radically minimizes our industrial carbon footprint.
