Exploration Technology

Advanced Geoscience Capabilities

Our upstream operations integrate sovereign-grade subsurface intelligence with multi-modal logistics planning—ensuring every discovery is commercially viable and logistically executable via marine terminals, rail-freight corridors, and trunk pipeline systems.

4D Seismic Imaging

Time-lapse acoustic waveform inversion monitors fluid migration and pressure gradients in real-time, enabling dynamic reservoir management across Yamal, East Siberia, and Arctic offshore blocks.

Petrophysical Core Analysis

Laboratory-grade micro-analysis of core samples determines absolute porosity matrices, permeability anisotropy, and fluid saturation profiles across all stratigraphic layers.

Integrated Logistics Modeling

Reservoir development plans are co-optimized with trunk pipeline routing, rail-freight capacity scheduling, and marine terminal berth allocation to ensure commercial readiness at first oil.

AI Reservoir Forecasting

Machine learning models trained on 15+ years of production data predict decline curves, water breakthrough timing, and optimal well placement with 94.2% accuracy.

Environmental Baseline Mapping

Pre-drill ecological surveys, permafrost stability modeling, and biodiversity impact assessments ensure full compliance with Russian Federal Law 7-FZ and international ESG standards.

Digital Twin Integration

Every asset is mirrored in a real-time digital twin that synchronizes subsurface data, surface facilities, and logistics corridors for unified operational decision-making.
